About Us
  • This service is designed to facilitate meal planning and shopping for busy families. Specifically lunch/dinner, since breakfast items tend to stay the same week to week.
  • Simply enter in your favorite recipes to create a database of recipes to make shopping lists from in the future.
  • Shuffle to create a new meal plan, and print out your shopping list.
  • Once you've built a set of recipes you like, you can come back any time and make a new meal plan and shopping list whenever you need one.
  • We will never sell or distribute your information to anyone. We take privacy very seriously. We only ask for an email address in order to enable account recovery.
  • Any questions, concerns, and feature suggestions are always welcome. You can get in touch via our contact page.
  • If you find this service useful, consider making a donation to ameliorate hosting costs.

How-To
  • 1. Create an Account.
  • 2. Confirm/Activate your account via email.
  • 3. Log in.
  • 4. Enter recipes you like to make from the 'Dashboard' using the 'Add Recipe' button. (for best results enter more recipes than the number of days you normally shop for at once) NOTE: This step is only required one time.
  • 5. Select the number of days you'd like to shop for this time from the dropdown box.
  • 6. Hit the 'Shuffle Recipes' button on the Dashboard until satisfied with the current mealplan.
  • 7. Hit the 'Get Shopping List' button on the Dashboard.
  • 8. Print or save your shopping list.
  • 9. Go Shop!
